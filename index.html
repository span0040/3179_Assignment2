World Happiness Report 2021


The World Happiness Report is an annual report that contains rankings and analysis of national happiness, based on respondent ratings of their own lives.

The main source of data is the Gallup World Poll, which contains life evaluation categories such as Logarithmized GDP per capita, Social Support, Healthy Life Expectancy, Freedom to make life choices, and Corruption perception from respondents from every country polled. These categories are then compiled and weighted to tabluate the Life Ladder Score.
